Recycling, Bins & Litter


This week you asked us about...

Did you know you can access most of our services 24/7 on i-Local? You can check what day we will collect your bins, if it's a blue or black bag week and download a calendar. Report a problem with litter or dog fouling or let us know if we've missed your bin collection. You can also keep up to date with changes to service by signing up for email/text alerts.
